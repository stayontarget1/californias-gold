# REGION: 99 / Yosemite Route
## Landmarks & Architecture
- [William S. Hart Park & Museum](https://maps.apple.com/?q=24151+Newhall+Ave+Newhall+CA+91321) *(Newhall — silent-era cowboy star's hilltop mansion & museum; free, with a small bison herd)*
- [Melody Ranch Motion Picture Studio](https://maps.apple.com/?q=24715+Oak+Creek+Ave+Santa+Clarita+CA+91321) *(Newhall — 1915 Western film-set town, Gunsmoke to Deadwood; exterior / tour days only)*
- [Fort Tejon State Historic Park](https://maps.apple.com/?q=4201+Fort+Tejon+Rd+Lebec+CA+93243) *(Lebec — 1854 U.S. Army frontier fort atop the Grapevine; living-history days)*
- [Bakersfield Fox Theater](https://maps.apple.com/?q=2001+H+St+Bakersfield+CA+93301) *(Bakersfield — 1930 Art Deco palace with the iconic neon marquee)*
- [Hanford Fox Theatre](https://maps.apple.com/?q=326+N+Irwin+St+Hanford+CA+93230) *(Hanford — 1929 marquee theater on the courthouse square)*
- [China Alley](https://maps.apple.com/?q=China+Alley+Hanford+CA+93230) *(Hanford — historic Chinatown with the 1893 Taoist Temple)*
- [Visalia Fox Theatre](https://maps.apple.com/?q=308+W+Main+St+Visalia+CA+93291) *(Visalia — 1930 Moorish/Spanish movie palace)*
- [Kingsburg Swedish Village](https://maps.apple.com/?q=Draper+St+Kingsburg+CA+93631) *(Kingsburg — Swedish-themed downtown; Dala horse and coffee-pot water tower)*
- [Forestiere Underground Gardens](https://maps.apple.com/?q=5021+W+Shaw+Ave+Fresno+CA+93722) *(Fresno — 10 acres of hand-dug Sicilian catacomb gardens, 1906–46; seasonal tours)*
- [Warnors Theatre](https://maps.apple.com/?q=1400+Fulton+St+Fresno+CA+93721) *(Fresno — 1928 Pantages movie palace with a thundering pipe organ)*
- [Tower Theatre](https://maps.apple.com/?q=815+E+Olive+Ave+Fresno+CA+93728) *(Fresno — 1939 Art Deco neon anchor of the Tower District)*
- [Yosemite Mountain Sugar Pine Railroad](https://maps.apple.com/?q=56001+Hwy+41+Fish+Camp+CA+93623) *(Fish Camp — narrow-gauge steam logging train, 1913/1928 locomotives; State Historic Landmark, seasonal)*
- [Mariposa County Courthouse](https://maps.apple.com/?q=5088+Bullion+St+Mariposa+CA+95338) *(Mariposa — 1854; oldest courthouse in continuous use west of the Mississippi)*
- [California State Mining & Mineral Museum](https://maps.apple.com/?q=5005+Fairgrounds+Rd+Mariposa+CA+95338) *(Mariposa — Gold Rush relics incl. the 14-lb Fricot Nugget)*
- [The Mainzer](https://maps.apple.com/?q=1110+Main+St+Merced+CA+95340) *(Merced — restored 1921 downtown theater)*
- [Castle Air Museum](https://maps.apple.com/?q=5050+Santa+Fe+Dr+Atwater+CA+95301) *(Atwater — big WWII/Cold War aircraft collection off Hwy 99)*
- [Railtown 1897 State Historic Park](https://maps.apple.com/?q=10501+Reservoir+Rd+Jamestown+CA+95327) *(Jamestown — Sierra Railway roundhouse + movie-star Engine No. 3)*
- [Oakdale Cowboy Museum](https://maps.apple.com/?q=355+E+F+St+Oakdale+CA+95361) *(Oakdale — "Cowboy Capital of the World" rodeo heritage)*
