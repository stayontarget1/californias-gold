# REGION: Antelope Valley
## Landmarks & Architecture
- [Aerospace Walk of Honor](https://maps.apple.com/?q=Lancaster+Blvd+and+Sierra+Hwy+Lancaster+CA+93534) *(The BLVD, Lancaster — sidewalk plaques honoring 100 test pilots; restored F-4 Phantom II)*
- [AF Plant 42 / Lockheed Skunk Works](https://maps.apple.com/?q=15th+St+E+Palmdale+CA+93550) *(Palmdale — loaner F-16A and F-104N flank the Skunk Works gate; exterior only)*
- [Antelope Valley Fair & Event Center](https://maps.apple.com/?q=2551+W+Avenue+H+Lancaster+CA+93536) *(Lancaster — 1938 grounds; 134 acres of mid-century county-fair Americana)*
- [Antelope Valley Indian Museum State Historic Park](https://maps.apple.com/?q=15701+E+Avenue+M+Lancaster+CA+93535) *(Lancaster — 1928 Swiss-chalet-meets-Hopi-pueblo built into Piute Butte; Sat–Sun)*
- [Blackbird Airpark](https://maps.apple.com/?q=2503+E+Avenue+P+Palmdale+CA+93550) *(Palmdale — world's only co-display of an SR-71A, A-12, D-21, U-2D; free Fri–Sun)*
- [Cedar Center for the Arts (MOAH:CEDAR)](https://maps.apple.com/?q=44857+Cedar+Ave+Lancaster+CA+93534) *(Lancaster — 1938 PWA-era Art Moderne civic complex)*
- [The Hangar (Lancaster Municipal Stadium)](https://maps.apple.com/?q=45116+Valley+Central+Way+Lancaster+CA+93536) *(Lancaster — NASA F/A-18 mounted at the entrance)*
- [Joe Davies Heritage Airpark](https://maps.apple.com/?q=2001+E+Avenue+P+Palmdale+CA+93550) *(Palmdale — 22 aircraft built and flown at Plant 42; free Fri–Sun)*
- [Lancaster Museum of Art & History (MOAH)](https://maps.apple.com/?q=665+W+Lancaster+Blvd+Lancaster+CA+93534) *(The BLVD, Lancaster — the high-desert cultural anchor; Tue–Sun)*
- [Llano del Rio Cooperative Colony Ruins](https://maps.apple.com/?q=16851+CA-138+Llano+CA+93544) *(Llano — stone walls of LA's most ambitious socialist utopia (1914–18))*
- [Los Angeles Aqueduct / California Aqueduct Crossover](https://maps.apple.com/?q=3+Points+Rd+Neenach+CA+93536) *(Neenach — Mulholland's 1913 pipe atop the 1971 State Water Project channel)*
- [St. Andrew's Abbey](https://maps.apple.com/?q=31001+N+Valyermo+Rd+Valyermo+CA+93563) *(Valyermo — 1955 Benedictine monastery; ceramics studio, retreat house)*
- [Western Hotel Museum](https://maps.apple.com/?q=557+W+Lancaster+Blvd+Lancaster+CA+93534) *(The BLVD, Lancaster — 1888 oldest surviving building in Lancaster)*

## Parks & Playgrounds
- [Antelope Valley California Poppy Reserve](https://maps.apple.com/?q=15101+Lancaster+Rd+Lancaster+CA+93536) *(west AV — the valley's signature bloom; March–April peak)*
- [Apollo Community Regional Park](https://maps.apple.com/?q=4555+W+Avenue+G+Lancaster+CA+93536) *(Lancaster — three lakes named Aldrin, Armstrong, Collins, 1972)*
- [Arthur B. Ripley Desert Woodland State Park](https://maps.apple.com/?q=Lancaster+Rd+and+210th+St+W+Lancaster+CA+93536) *(west of Lancaster — preserved virgin Joshua tree forest; free)*
- [Big Rock Creek Recreation Area](https://maps.apple.com/?q=Big+Rock+Creek+Rd+Valyermo+CA+93563) *(Valyermo — year-round creek; dry-camp swimming holes)*
- [Devil's Punchbowl Natural Area](https://maps.apple.com/?q=28000+Devils+Punchbowl+Rd+Pearblossom+CA+93553) *(Pearblossom — tilted San Andreas sandstone slabs)*
- [Musical Road (Honda's "Lone Ranger" rumble strips)](https://maps.apple.com/?q=Avenue+G+and+35th+St+W+Lancaster+CA+93536) *(Lancaster — drive 55 mph and the grooved pavement plays the William Tell Overture)*
- [Prime Desert Woodland Preserve](https://maps.apple.com/?q=43201+35th+St+W+Lancaster+CA+93536) *(Lancaster — in-town Joshua tree woodland with interpretive boardwalk; free)*
- [Saddleback Butte State Park](https://maps.apple.com/?q=17150+E+Ave+J+Lancaster+CA+93535) *(near Lake LA — 1960 park; 3,000-ft granite butte)*
- [Vasquez Rocks Natural Area & Nature Center](https://maps.apple.com/?q=10700+W+Escondido+Canyon+Rd+Agua+Dulce+CA+91350) *(Agua Dulce — the tilted sandstone slabs of Star Trek and Westerns; free)*
